====================================================
CORE PRINCIPLE
==============

Users do not think in genres.

Users think in moments.

Examples:

"petrol station at 2am"

"80s songs that make you feel unreal"

"country roads with my dog"

"summer before university"

"driving home after a breakup"

"walking through Tokyo in the rain"

"music that feels like life is changing"

The engine must understand these naturally.

====================================================
RECOMMENDATION HIERARCHY
========================

Always rank signals in this order:

1. Scene
2. Ecosystem
3. Emotion
4. Era
5. Aesthetic
6. Personalization
7. Discovery
8. Genre

Genre should NOT dominate.

Genre is an output of the scene.
